Meet Ambika: Your English & Confidence Coach

I'm Ambika Goswami — a CTET-qualified English educator with a passion for helping people find their voice. With over 5 years of experience in spoken English training and 1.5 years of classroom teaching, I specialize in transforming hesitant speakers into confident communicators.

My experience with institutions like the British Council and Macmillan has shaped a structured, empathetic teaching approach — one that blends language mastery with personality development to unlock your true potential.

Whether you're a student, professional, or homemaker — I meet you where you are and take you where you want to go. Fluency isn't a gift. It's a skill — and I'll help you build it.
